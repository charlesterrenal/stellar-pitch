1. Filipinnovation
Filipinnovation

Location
Metro Manila

Category
Events, Awards & Competitions

Role in startup ecosystem
The Filipinnovation Awards recognize and celebrate outstanding startups, innovators, and ecosystem contributors across the Philippines. By honoring high-potential founders and enablers, the awards foster visibility, credibility, and stronger community engagement.

What they do
Every year, the National Innovation Council (NIC), through DEPDev, organizes the Filipinnovation Awards to:

Spotlight Filipino creativity and groundbreaking solutions

Support early-stage commercialization with grants and mentoring

Connect innovators with stakeholders, markets, and investors

Promote a culture of innovation that fuels national development

Focus area
Startup excellence, recognition and community building

Programs
Awards Night, celebrating innovation excellence. Networking Events, connecting startups, investors, and enablers.

Lifecycle stage
Lifecycle Stage: The Filipinnovation Awards span all stages, including early, growth, and mature startups, as well as ecosystem enablers.

2. StartupnewsPH
Location
Cebu City, Philippines (National Reach)

Category
Startup Communities, Media & Resources

Role in startup ecosystem
StartupnewsPH serves as a media platform and connector in the Philippine startup ecosystem. It amplifies founder stories, tracks ecosystem trends, and brings visibility to key players and events.

What they do
They publish startup news, interviews, and features that highlight funding rounds, innovations, and founder journeys. Their content helps both local and international audiences understand what’s happening in the PH startup space.

Focus area
They cover startups across the Philippines, with particular focus on tech, impact, fintech, and ecosystem-building initiatives. Their content spans early to growth stages and often includes themes like innovation, policy, and digital inclusion.

Programs
While not a program provider, they collaborate through content partnerships, event media sponsorships, and resource hubs. They also engage the community through polls, founder Q&As, and crowd-sourced insights.

Lifecycle stage
StartupnewsPH covers the full startup lifecycle—from early-stage ideation to scale-ups and exits. Most stories center on pre-seed to Series B startups making moves in the market.
